## Customer

- third-party claims administrators (TPAs)
- average adjusters
- insurance-consultancy firms

## Offer

- outsourced claims-administration intake
- average-adjustment / damage-apportionment proposal
- recommendation report
- immutable audit ledger

## Revenue

- self-host setup: one-time implementation fee
- managed hosting: monthly subscription per case volume
- support: monthly retainer with SLA
- migration: import from an incumbent claims-administration system
- per-case fee

## Trust Controls

- no claims-administration recommendation or average adjustment is finalized without human sign-off
- a fabricated apportionment forces a hold, not an override
- every recommendation path is auditable
- emergency manual override paths remain outside LLM control
